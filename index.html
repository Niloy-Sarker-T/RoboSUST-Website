RoboSUST is a robotics based organisation of Shahjalal University of Science and Technology(SUST) aimed to make an open platform for students of all department who wish to spend time with robot and robotics. Starting from 2015 RoboSUST has 60+ achievements and our very own Ribo and Lee (humanoid robot) till now.

Ribo

RIBO is the first social interaction robot in Bangladesh. This robot is designed and developed by 'ROBO SUST' team of Shahjalal University of Science and Technology. RIBO is able to hands and eyes ups and downs, can walk very slowly and can speak some Bengali recorded sentences.

Li

Li can walk, understands and speaks Bangla, can identify faces, mimic expressions and answer questions regarding Bangladesh and the Liberation War. The robot was made by a five-member team called 'Friday Lab' led by former SUST student and lecturer of North-East University, Nowshad Sajib putting in three years of frantic effort costing around Tk10 lakh.
